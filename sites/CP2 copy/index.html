About

Hi, I'm Mike, a web designer & developer based in Austin, TX. I am passionate about creating engaging and cohesive web experiences that inform and entertain.
After an 18 year career at Apple as a QA Manager, I decided to move to Austin and persue a new path. I attended Austin Coding Academy to become a web designer and developer.
My time at Apple taught me that user experience should rise above all other factors. I use this as my guiding principle.
"What we make testifies who we are. People can sense care and can sense carelessness. This relates to respect for each other and carelessness is personally offensive."
-Jonathan Ive
